Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EU Quiz App handles information when you use the app and this website.

By using the app or this website, you agree to the handling of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app or website.

1. Who is responsible for the app?

Developer: Max Riedel
Contact: eu.quiz.app@gmail.com

2. What the app does

EU Quiz App is an independent educational app for practicing EU-related quiz questions. The app offers free content and a paid upgrade that unlocks additional features and content.

3. Information processed by the app

The app may process the following categories of information:

  • App preferences and local settings, such as the selected app language and locally stored bookmarks.
  • Purchase information, such as whether a premium entitlement is active, as provided through Apple’s in-app purchase systems.
  • Advertising-related requests and identifiers for banner and interstitial ads when the free version of the app is used. Depending on your device settings, permissions, region, and consent choices, this may include advertising identifiers or other device-related information used for ad delivery, frequency control, fraud prevention, measurement, and privacy choices.
  • Support communications you voluntarily send by email.
  • Technical diagnostics you choose to send, such as device model, iOS version, app version, and locale when using the app’s support or feedback email flow.
  • Website information, such as technical server or browser information that may be processed when you visit this website, depending on your hosting provider and standard web infrastructure.

The developer does not seek to collect more personal data than necessary. In particular, payment card details are not collected directly by the developer.

4. Data stored on your device

Certain information is stored locally on your device to enable app functionality, such as language preference and bookmarked questions. This local storage is used to provide the app experience and is not intended as advertising tracking.

5. How information is used

Information may be used to:

  • provide, operate, and maintain the app and website;
  • enable core app features such as quiz progress, preferences, and bookmarks;
  • determine whether premium access is active;
  • display ads in the free version of the app;
  • respond to support or feedback messages;
  • troubleshoot technical issues, improve reliability, and protect against abuse or misuse;
  • comply with legal obligations.

6. Website usage

When you visit this website, basic technical information may be processed automatically by the hosting provider (e.g., IP address, browser type, and access time) in order to deliver the website and ensure its security and stability.

This website does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics tools for user tracking or profiling.

No personal data is actively collected through the website unless you choose to contact the developer (for example, via email links).

7. Advertising

The free version of the app may display ads delivered by Google Mobile Ads / AdMob. Those third-party services may process device or advertising-related information in accordance with their own privacy documentation and your device permissions and settings.

Depending on your location, device settings, and consent choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized. Where required, the app may present consent choices before requesting ads. You can also manage certain privacy and advertising choices through your device settings and, where available, through the app’s privacy options.

8. In-app purchases

Premium purchases are handled through Apple’s App Store infrastructure. The developer does not receive your full payment card details. Apple may share limited transaction and entitlement information necessary to determine whether premium access is active.

9. Email support

If you contact support, the developer will process the information you include in your message in order to respond to your request, troubleshoot problems, and improve support quality.

If you use the app’s built-in support or feedback email flow, your message may also include technical diagnostics you choose to send, such as device model, iOS version, app version, and locale.

10. Legal bases

Where EU/EEA data protection law applies, processing may rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • performance of a contract or steps requested by you;
  • legitimate interests in operating, securing, supporting, and improving the app and website;
  • your consent, where required by law, platform rules, or device settings, including certain advertising-related processing;
  • compliance with legal obligations.

11. Sharing of information

The developer may share information only as needed with service providers involved in operating the app, such as:

  • Apple, for app distribution and in-app purchases;
  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads, for advertising delivery in the free version;
  • email providers, for handling support communications;
  • website hosting or infrastructure providers, if relevant for this website.

These third parties may process data under their own privacy policies and terms. The developer does not sell your personal data.

12. International transfers

Some third-party service providers may process information outside your country of residence. Where applicable, such transfers are carried out subject to the safeguards provided under applicable data protection law.

Support emails and related communications may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to the request, maintain records, resolve disputes, improve support quality, and comply with legal obligations.

Data stored locally on your device remains there until you remove the app, clear the relevant local data, or change the stored settings. Advertising and purchase-related retention may also be determined by the relevant third-party providers under their own policies.

14. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability regarding your personal data, as well as the right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise privacy-related rights, please contact eu.quiz.app@gmail.com.

The developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children where such collection would require parental consent. If you believe that personal data from a child has been provided inappropriately, please make contact using the email address below.
